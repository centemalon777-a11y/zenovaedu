Meet the Founder

The Vision Behind Zenova

Emmanuel John founded Zenova in February 2026 with one clear goal — to close the educational gap for Nigerian students. Frustrated by the inequality in access to quality learning resources, he set out to build a platform that gives every student, regardless of their background, the tools they need to succeed.

Under his leadership, Zenova has launched multiple products — from JAMB prep platforms to nationwide CBT competitions — all built with a mobile-first, student-first mindset.

"I wanted something that will give students the best education and also make the education available for all students — no inequality."
